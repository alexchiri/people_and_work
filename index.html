Notes on people and work

A presentation with borrowed thoughts on people and work

Created by Alexandru Chirițescu / @alexchiri

Thank you all for joining! I have to admit, I changed the introduction and the title of this presentation several times. I didn’t want to sound like I know all the answers or like I’m criticising or giving lessons. Far from me those thoughts! Consider this presentation to be a conversation starter. From my general working experience, I noticed that the human part of “work” tends to get overlooked. I consider the human aspect essential in accomplishing anything. Like it or not, everyone has feelings, opinions or desires. Nowadays, words or expressions like passion, values, leaders and leadership, work / personal life balance, trust or communication are presented like the secret ingredients in creating a successful organization. I want to use the next 10-15 minutes to share some of my thoughts or things that I picked up about some of these topics.
I structured my presentation as a Top 10, so if I suck really bad, at least you can track progress! And if that's not enough, just follow this beige line at the bottom! As you see, I want to make sure you're at least entertained. But let’s get started!
